## Full Description
SeaWise Voyager is a port-to-port distance and fuel consumption calculator built for maritime professionals. Enter your origin and destination ports, configure your vessel's speed and burn rate, and get precise calculations for distance in nautical miles, total fuel consumption, estimated travel time, and voyage cost.

Key Features:

● Distance & Fuel Calculator — Calculate port-to-port distance with fuel consumption based on your vessel's fuel curve. Get results in nautical miles, liters, hours, and dollars.

● AI Sea Route Planning — Auto-generate maritime routes that follow real shipping lanes, navigate through straits and canals, and stay on water. Powered by Google Gemini with Google Search grounding for accurate waypoints.

● Global Fuel Market — Track bunker fuel prices at major ports worldwide including Rotterdam, Singapore, Fujairah, Houston, and Gibraltar. AI-powered price sync with supplier information.

● Vessel Profile — Configure vessel specifications including fuel curve (RPM, speed, burn rate), total fuel capacity, and measurement units. Analyze fuel efficiency with performance visualization.

● Trip Catalog — Save and review voyage history with distance, fuel, and cost analytics. Track spending trends over time.

● Cloud Sync — Access your vessel profiles, saved routes, and trip history across all your devices.

Built by JK Yacht Ops for captains, fleet managers, and maritime operations teams who need accurate voyage planning down to the nearest mile, minute, and dollar.
